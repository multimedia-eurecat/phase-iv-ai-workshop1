Welcome to the 1st Workshop on Clinical-Technical Collaboration for Research and Development of Artificial Intelligence (AI) Services in Health Data, which will take place on April 11, 2024 from 9:00-13:30 at Vall d'Hebron Research Institute (VHIR) - Collserola building.

IMPORTANT: Participants must confirm their attendance before April 8.

This workshop will discuss AI services for the lung cancer use case. The objective is to evaluate the impact of AI on diagnostic accuracy and cost, radiologists' workload and clinical outcomes. The event will bring together healthcare professionals and AI researchers to explore innovative image-based ways to improve efficiency and support in healthcare processes.

This is an opportunity to share knowledge between two fields that often work too independently on common problems. By joining forces, we believe that we can drive significant advances in the field of digital health and improve the quality of life of patients. It will also be an excellent occasion to strengthen existing collaborations, as well as to foster future partnerships and innovative projects.

Organizers

This workshop is part of the PHASE IV AI European project and is organized by

  • Vall d'Hebron Research Institute (VHIR)

    Created in 1994, the VHIR promotes and develops biosanitary research, innovation and teaching at the Vall d'Hebron University Hospital in Barcelona. Its mission is to identify and apply new solutions to the health problems of society, achieving leadership and excellence at national and international level.

  • Eurecat - Technology Centre of Catalonia

    Formed in 2015, Eurecat is the leading technology partner for companies working in Catalonia. It provides the industrial and business sector with differential technology and advanced knowledge, offering solutions to innovation needs and boosting competitiveness in a changing environment.
