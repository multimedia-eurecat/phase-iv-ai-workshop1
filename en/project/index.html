PHASE IV AI

The European project PHASE IV AI aims to provide and validate a platform of scientific and technological results accessible as online services in the field of digital health. The accessibility and applicability of such results are key issues for the competitiveness of the European healthcare industry.

PHASE IV AI will provide AI data synthesis services (DaaS), AI modeling services (MaaS) and a new health data center (Data Hub). The aim is for health services and healthcare providers to have access to high-quality data and computing capabilities that ensure data privacy. This is intended to assist in diagnosis, as well as to reduce development times in the innovation of health data-driven products.
